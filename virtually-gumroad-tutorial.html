What is Gumroad?

The Simplest Way to Sell What You Know

Gumroad is a direct-to-buyer platform that lets you sell digital products, courses, and memberships without needing a website. Perfect for VAs, creators, and coaches.

⚡
Zero Technical Skills Required

Upload a file, set a price, share a link. Your product can be live and earning within 10 minutes of signing up.

💸
No Monthly Subscription

Gumroad only charges 10% per sale. There are zero upfront or monthly costs — you pay only when you earn.

🌍
Built-in Global Audience

Gumroad's discover feed exposes your products to millions of active buyers — organic traffic included from day one.

Tutorial

Your Complete Gumroad Walkthrough

Open Gumroad ↗
01
Setup
Account Creation
Create Your Gumroad Account

Gumroad is completely free to join. All you need is an email address — your storefront can be live in under 10 minutes.

A
Go to gumroad.com

Visit gumroad.com and click Start Selling in the top right corner of the homepage.

B
Sign up with Email or Google

Enter your email + password, or use "Continue with Google" for a one-click faster sign-up.

C
Verify your email address

Check your inbox for Gumroad's verification link. Click it to fully activate your account and unlock all features.

D
Complete the onboarding quiz

Gumroad asks what type of creator you are. Choose the category that best describes your digital products or services.

✦ VirtuAlly Tip: Use a professional email that matches your VA brand domain — it builds credibility with buyers at checkout.
Sign Up Free →
02
Setup
Profile & Storefront
Build Your Creator Profile

Your Gumroad profile is your storefront. A polished profile builds trust, communicates credibility, and converts visitors into paying buyers.

A
Upload a professional photo

Use a high-quality headshot or branded logo. Minimum 400×400px. Square images display best.

B
Write your creator bio

2–3 sentences: who you are, what you create, and who it's for. Speak directly to your ideal buyer's needs and goals.

C
Set your custom URL

Go to Settings → Profile to claim gumroad.com/yourname. Keep it short, brand-consistent, and easy to share.

D
Add social links + cover image

Link your website, Instagram, or LinkedIn. Add a cover banner (1500×500px) to make your page feel like a real brand.

✦ Profile Checklist: Photo ✓ · Bio ✓ · Custom URL ✓ · Social links ✓ · Cover banner ✓
03
Products
Product Creation
Create Your First Digital Product

Gumroad supports a wide range of digital product types. From your dashboard, click + New Product to get started building your first listing.

📄 File Products

  • PDF guides & templates
  • Excel / Google Sheets
  • Canva templates (PDF/PNG)
  • eBooks & workbooks
  • Swipe files & scripts

🎥 Courses & Content

  • Video lessons (MP4)
  • Audio courses (MP3)
  • Mini-courses & workshops
  • Membership content
  • Coaching programs
1
Choose your product type

Select: Digital Product, Course, Membership, Bundle, or Physical. Most VAs will use Digital Product or Course.

2
Write a compelling name & description

Lead with the transformation or outcome. Use benefit-focused language. Include keywords your buyer would search.

3
Upload your product files

Drag and drop files directly. Gumroad supports PDF, ZIP, MP4, MP3, EPUB, PSD, and more. Max file size is 16GB per product.

4
Design your cover image

Create a 1280×720px (16:9) cover in Canva. Your cover is the #1 factor that drives clicks — invest real time here.

Products
Pricing Strategy
Set Your Price & Offer Types

Gumroad gives you flexible pricing options — one-time purchases, "pay what you want," recurring subscriptions, and free lead magnets to grow your list.

💰 Pricing Options

  • Free (lead magnet / list building)
  • Fixed price (one-time payment)
  • Pay What You Want (set minimum)
  • Subscription / Membership

🎁 Offer Boosters

  • Discount codes (e.g. LAUNCH30)
  • Bundle products together
  • Upsell / cross-sell links
  • Quantity-limited deals
A
Create a launch discount code

Go to Checkout → Offer Codes. Create a code like LAUNCH30 for 30% off. Use it in your first promotional posts to drive early sales.

B
Set up a subscription product

Select the "Membership" product type to charge monthly or yearly. Great for recurring templates, newsletters, or ongoing resources.

✦ Pricing Tip: A $7 product often outsells a $5 product because it signals higher value. Avoid pricing below $5 — Gumroad's 10% fee eats into very small margins.
05
Payments
Payment Setup
Connect Your Bank & Get Paid

Before going live, connect your bank account or PayPal so Gumroad knows exactly where to send your earnings. Go to Settings → Payouts to configure this.

1
Navigate to Settings → Payouts

Click your profile icon in the Gumroad dashboard, choose Settings, then select the "Payouts" tab.

2
Connect your bank account

US sellers: add your bank routing + account number. International sellers: connect via PayPal or a Stripe-supported bank.

3
Submit tax information

US sellers submit a W-9 (SSN or EIN). International sellers submit a W-8BEN form. Gumroad collects and remits applicable taxes on your behalf.

4
Understand the full fee structure

Gumroad charges a flat 10% per sale. Payment processing adds ~2.9% + $0.30 per transaction. No monthly fee, ever.

💳 Accepted Payments

  • Visa / Mastercard / Amex
  • PayPal
  • Apple Pay / Google Pay
  • International cards

📅 Payout Schedule

  • Weekly every Friday
  • Minimum $10 payout threshold
  • First payout held 7 days (anti-fraud)
  • Direct bank or PayPal transfer
06
Selling
Publishing & Sharing
Publish & Share Your Product

Once your product looks good in preview, toggle it to Published and start sharing your link. Gumroad gives you multiple ways to reach buyers — use all of them.

A
Preview before publishing

Click "Preview" in the product editor to see exactly what buyers see. Always check mobile view — over 60% of buyers shop on mobile.

B
Toggle from Draft → Published

Your product is now live at its unique Gumroad URL. Copy the link immediately: gumroad.com/l/your-product

C
Embed a checkout button on your website

Use Gumroad's overlay embed code so buyers can checkout without leaving your site. Paste it into any webpage with one line of HTML.

D
Share across all channels

Drop your link in your Instagram bio (Linktree or direct), email newsletter, Pinterest pins, LinkedIn posts, and Facebook groups.

🚀 Launch Tip: Don't wait for perfection. Get your first product live, gather real buyer feedback, and iterate from there. Done beats perfect every time.
Go Live →
07
Growth
Growth & Analytics
Manage Sales & Grow Your Store

After your first sale, use Gumroad's built-in tools to track performance, email your buyers, build bundles, and turn one sale into many recurring ones.

1
Monitor your Analytics Dashboard

Go to Analytics to track views, conversions, revenue over time, and which traffic sources send buyers. Review weekly.

2
Email your past buyers (for free)

Use Gumroad's built-in email tool to send updates, new product drops, or exclusive offers to your buyer list — zero extra cost.

3
Collect reviews to boost conversions

After purchase, Gumroad auto-emails buyers for a review. Positive reviews dramatically increase conversion rates on your page.

4
Bundle products for higher AOV

Once you have 2+ products, create a bundle at a discount. Bundles increase average order value and make value obvious to buyers.

5
Launch an affiliate program

Go to your product → Affiliates and let others promote for a commission you control. Automated passive promotion at zero extra effort.

📈 Growth Formula: 1 hero product + email list growth + consistent content = compounding Gumroad revenue. Your first $100 is the hardest. Stay consistent.
Pro Tips

6 Things That Make Your Store Succeed

These are the non-obvious moves that separate top-performing Gumroad stores from ones that go silent after launch.

01
🎨
Invest in Your Cover Image

Your cover is your product's billboard. A Canva-designed 1280×720px cover can double click-through rates versus a plain screenshot or no image.

02
📧
Start Free, Build Your List

Offer one product for free to collect email subscribers. Then sell to that warm list when you launch your paid products. Email converts 3–5× better than social.

03
💬
Use Social Proof Early

Send your product to 3–5 friends or beta users for free, ask for an honest review, and add those reviews to your listing before your public launch.

04
🔁
Create a Product Suite

Plan a starter product ($7–$25), a core offer ($47–$97), and a premium bundle ($150+). Give buyers a natural upgrade path within your store.

05
📌
Pin Links Everywhere

Put your Gumroad store link in your Instagram bio, LinkedIn About, TikTok bio, email signature, and every content piece you publish. Visibility = sales.

06
🔄
Update Products Regularly

Improve and re-release your digital products. Existing buyers get the update free — this creates goodwill, word-of-mouth, and repeat purchases.
