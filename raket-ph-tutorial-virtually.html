Part 1

Creating Your Account

Getting started on Raket PH takes less than 5 minutes. Here's exactly how to sign up and log in.

1

Go to Raket PH Website

Open your browser and navigate to raket.ph. The site works on both desktop and mobile. Look for the "Sign Up" or "Get Started" button on the homepage — it's usually prominently placed at the top or center of the page.

2

Choose Your Sign-Up Method

You have two options for registering your account — choose whichever is easiest for you.

📧 Email + Password 🔵 Continue with Google 📘 Continue with Facebook
💡 Using Google sign-in is the fastest option — no password to remember and your email is already verified automatically.
3

Fill In Your Registration Details

If signing up with email, enter your full name (or your brand/business name), your email address, and create a strong password — at least 8 characters mixing uppercase letters, numbers, and symbols. Double-check your email spelling before submitting.

4

Verify Your Email Address

After submitting your registration, Raket PH will send a verification email to your inbox. Open it and click the confirmation link to activate your account. This is a required step before you can list or sell anything.

⚠️ If you don't see the email within a few minutes, check your Spam or Junk folder. You can also request a resend from the login page.
5

Log In to Your New Account

Once verified, return to raket.ph and click "Log In". Enter your email and password — or click the Google/Facebook button if you used social sign-in. You'll land on your Dashboard, which is your seller command center.

💡 Bookmark raket.ph or save your login details in a password manager so you can easily access your dashboard every time.
Part 2

Setting Up Your Seller Profile

Your profile is your storefront. A complete, trustworthy profile converts more browsers into buyers — don't skip this step.

1

Navigate to Account Settings

Once logged in, look for your profile icon or your name in the top-right corner of the dashboard. Click it and select "Account Settings" or "Edit Profile" to open your seller profile editor.

2

Upload Your Profile Photo

Add a clear, professional-looking profile photo or your brand logo. A real face photo tends to build more personal trust with Filipino buyers. Recommended dimensions: 400×400px minimum in JPG or PNG format.

💡 Profiles with a real photo receive significantly more clicks than those showing a generic avatar. It's one of the highest-impact things you can do.
3

Choose Your Username & Store URL

Select a unique username that's memorable and brand-friendly. This becomes your public store URL — for example: raket.ph/your-username. Keep it short, professional, and consistent with your other social media handles so buyers can find you easily.

⚠️ Choose your username carefully — changing it later may affect your store URL and any links you've already shared.
4

Write a Compelling Bio

Your bio is your elevator pitch. Tell buyers who you are, what type of digital products you create, and why they should trust your work. Keep it short, friendly, and specific. Here's a great formula:

✍️ "I create [product type] for [target audience] so they can [benefit]. Based in the Philippines."

Example: "I create Canva templates for Filipino small business owners so they can look professional online — even with zero design experience."
5

Link Your Social Media Accounts

Connect your Facebook, Instagram, TikTok, or LinkedIn pages to your Raket PH profile. This is optional but highly recommended — many Filipino buyers will check your socials before deciding to purchase, especially for higher-priced products.

6

Save Your Profile

Review everything one more time, then click "Save" or "Update Profile". Visit your public store URL to see exactly how buyers will see your page. Does it look complete and trustworthy? If yes, you're ready for the next step!

Part 3

Creating Your First Listing

This is the most important part. A well-crafted listing is the difference between a product that sells and one that gets ignored.

What you can sell on Raket PH

📄E-books / PDFs
🎨Canva Templates
🎓Online Courses
📸Photo Presets
📊Notion/Excel Templates
🖋️Fonts & Brushes
🖼️Digital Prints
🎵Music / Beats
💻Code / Plugins
📋Planners & Worksheets
1

Click "Add New Product" or "Create Listing"

From your seller dashboard, look for the button to create a new product — it may say "Add Product," "New Listing," or "Sell Something." Click it to open the product creation form.

2

Write a Keyword-Rich Product Title

Your title is the first thing buyers (and Raket PH search) will see. Make it descriptive, specific, and searchable. Mention what the product is, who it's for, and what format it comes in.

✍️ Weak: "Instagram Templates"
Strong: "50 Canva Instagram Story Templates for Filipino Small Businesses — Editable, Ready to Post"
Include the format Mention your audience Add a benefit
3

Write a Detailed Product Description

Your description needs to answer every question a buyer might have before they buy. Since they can't touch or try your product, your words and visuals do all the selling. Cover all of the following:

What's included (file list) Who it's for How to use it Software required File formats (PDF, ZIP, etc.) Number of files
💡 Use bullet points, short paragraphs, and bold text to make your description easy to scan. Most buyers won't read a wall of text.
4

Upload Your Digital Files

This is the actual product your buyer receives after purchase. Upload your final files — Raket PH will automatically deliver them when someone buys. If you have multiple files, compress them into a single ZIP folder for clean delivery.

PDF ZIP MP4 PSD / AI XLSX / DOCX PNG / JPG
🚨 Critical: Always test your files before uploading. Open the ZIP, check every file opens correctly. Broken downloads = refund requests + bad reviews.
5

Add High-Quality Cover Images / Mockups

Upload at least 3 professional product images. These are your most powerful selling tool — buyers judge your product by how it looks. Use mockup generators to show your product in context (e.g., a template displayed on a phone or laptop).

Recommended: 1200×800px Show product in use Preview inside pages/files 3+ images minimum
💡 Free mockup tools: Smartmockups, Mockup World, Creative Fabrica, or Anthony Boyd Graphics. Professional visuals dramatically increase click-through rates.
6

Set Your Price in Philippine Peso (₱)

Research what similar products are selling for on Raket PH, then price competitively. Don't undersell yourself — start at a fair price and adjust as you earn reviews. Here are typical price ranges to guide you:

E-books / PDFs
₱99 – ₱499
Templates
₱149 – ₱999
Courses / Bundles
₱299 – ₱2,999+
7

Select Category & Add Searchable Tags

Choose the most relevant category for your product from Raket PH's list. Then add tags — keywords buyers would type when searching for products like yours. Think from the buyer's perspective, not the seller's.

💡 Example tags for a Canva template: canva, instagram templates, social media, small business, filipina, content creator, editable templates
8

Preview, Review & Publish Your Listing

Before hitting publish, use the preview option to see exactly how your listing looks to buyers. Read through your title, description, and check all images. Once satisfied, click "Publish" — your product is now live on the marketplace!

🎉 Congratulations! Your first product is live. Share the link on your social media immediately to drive your first traffic.
Part 4

Payment Setup & Receiving Orders

Set up your payout method so you're ready to receive money from day one. This only takes a few minutes.

1

Go to Payment or Payout Settings

In your seller dashboard, find Settings → Payment Settings or Payout Settings. This is where you tell Raket PH where to send your earnings when buyers purchase from you.

2

Link Your Preferred Payout Method

Raket PH supports local Filipino payment methods, making it easy to receive your money. Enter your account details accurately — a single wrong digit can delay or misdirect your payout.

📱 GCash 💳 Maya / PayMaya 🏦 Local Bank Transfer
🚨 Double-check your account name and number before saving. Errors in payout details are the #1 reason sellers experience delayed payments.
3

Understand Platform Fees

Raket PH charges a platform/transaction fee on each sale. Your net earnings = your listed price minus this fee. Always check the current fee structure on the Raket PH help or pricing page, as rates can change. Factor this into your pricing from the start.

⚠️ Always verify Raket PH's latest fee structure directly on their platform — policies are updated periodically and this guide may not reflect the most current rates.
4

How the Buying Process Works (Automatic!)

Once your listing is live, the entire buying and delivery process is fully automated by Raket PH. Here's what happens when someone buys your product:

1. Buyer pays via GCash / card / online banking 2. Payment is confirmed automatically 3. Raket PH sends buyer a download link instantly 4. Earnings are added to your balance
💡 You don't need to manually send files or confirm orders — Raket PH handles all delivery automatically. You earn even while you sleep!
Part 5

Promote Your Products & Drive Sales

Listing is just the beginning. Here's how to get eyeballs on your product and turn views into actual sales.

1

Share Immediately on Social Media

The moment your listing goes live, post about it on Facebook, Instagram, TikTok, and Twitter/X. Create a short video or reel showing your product — video content gets organic reach that static posts simply can't match. Include your Raket PH store link in your bio.

2

Post in Relevant Filipino Facebook Groups

There are thousands of active Filipino communities where your target buyers spend time. Join and genuinely participate in relevant groups, then share your product when the rules allow. Always provide value first — don't just drop links.

Freelancing PH Negosyo Tips PH Work From Home PH OFW Opportunities Content Creators PH
3

Add Your Store Link to All Your Bios

Your Raket PH store URL (raket.ph/your-username) should be visible everywhere — your Instagram bio, Facebook page about section, email signature, TikTok profile, and even in your personal chat status. Every touchpoint is a potential sale.

💡 Use a link-in-bio tool like Linktree or Beacons to showcase all your products alongside your social profiles in one clean link.
4

Run a Launch Promo or Offer a Freebie

When you're just starting, first reviews are gold. Offer a limited-time discount, a free sample of your product, or a bonus for early buyers. Getting those first few purchases and reviews will snowball into organic sales — buyers trust products with reviews.

5

Create Educational Content Around Your Product

The most effective long-term strategy: create free tutorials, how-to videos, or blog posts that naturally lead to your paid product. Teach people something valuable, then mention your product as the tool that makes it easier.

✍️ Example: "How to create a professional Instagram feed for your Filipino small business" → at the end, promote your Canva template pack on Raket PH.
Part 6

Requesting Your Payout

You've made sales — now it's time to actually get paid. Here's how the withdrawal process works on Raket PH.

1

Check Your Earnings Dashboard

Go to Dashboard → Earnings or Sales to see your running balance, all completed orders, and your total revenue. Keep an eye on which products are performing best — this data tells you what to create more of.

2

Meet the Minimum Payout Threshold

Raket PH may require a minimum balance before you can request a withdrawal — for example, ₱100 to ₱500 (verify the current threshold on the platform). Keep selling until your available balance reaches this amount.

⚠️ Always verify the current minimum payout threshold directly on Raket PH — it may have been updated since this guide was published.
3

Click "Withdraw" or "Request Payout"

When your balance is ready, find the "Withdraw" button in your earnings or wallet section. Select your linked payout method (GCash, Maya, or bank), enter the amount you want to withdraw, and confirm the request.

4

Wait for Processing & Receive Your Money

Raket PH typically processes payouts within 1–5 business days, depending on your chosen method and their current payout schedule. You'll receive a notification once the transfer is sent.

GCash: Usually fastest (1–2 days) Bank Transfer: 3–5 business days
🎉 Your first payout is a milestone! Reinvest a portion into improving your existing products or creating new ones to grow your passive income stream.

Bonus

Pro Tips from @VirtuAlly

These are the habits and strategies that separate top-earning Raket PH sellers from those who never gain traction. Apply them from day one.

📦

List Multiple Products

Sellers with 5+ products consistently earn more than those with one. Each new listing is another entry point for buyers to discover your store.

⭐

Actively Collect Reviews

After every sale, follow up with buyers and kindly ask for a review. Social proof is your #1 conversion tool — even two or three good reviews can double your sales.

🔄

Update Your Products Regularly

Refresh your listings with updated files, new bonus content, or improved visuals. Active sellers get more visibility in Raket PH's algorithm.

📊

Study Your Analytics

Check which products get the most views vs. the most sales. High views + low sales = fix your description or images. Low views = improve your title and tags.

🎁

Create Product Bundles

Package 3–5 related products together at a slight discount. Bundles increase your average order value and give buyers a feeling of getting a great deal.

⚡

Reply to Questions Fast

When buyers message you with questions before buying, a fast, friendly reply is often what closes the sale. Slow responses lose sales to competitors.

💰

Price With Confidence

Start at a fair price, not the lowest. As you earn reviews and trust, gradually increase your prices. Underpricing can actually signal low quality to buyers.

🧪

Test Files Before Publishing

Always open your ZIPs and verify every file works before publishing. Broken downloads result in refund requests and negative reviews that are hard to recover from.
